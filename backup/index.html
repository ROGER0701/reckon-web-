Reckon 3.0

The third edition of RECKON is back, and we look forward to welcoming all the creative and innovative minds to participate in this 36-hour hackathon taking place at Jodhpur Institute of Engineering and Technology to hack and have fun by solving some real-world problems.RECKON provides you with the most precise platform if you believe in the power of transforming ideas into solutions. Our vision is to make students believe in their ideas and fabricate reality.

RECKON 1.0 was the first of its kind, the adrenaline and enthusiasm of students were at the notch and the theme of the hackathon was “Social Hackathon”. We received an outstanding level of participation from developers all across the region.

Inspired by the success of RECKON 1.0., RECKON 2.0 was organized in 2019 at a larger scale and the theme was “Social Entrepreneurship”. The count of participants increased as the hackathon became a national level event. RECKON 2.0 happened to be the biggest hackathon Jodhpur has ever witnessed.

General Information

A hackathon is an invention marathon. Students come together in teams to build cool software and hardware projects over the course of 36 hours.

Registration are open to all.Students(school/college),professionals,startups etc can surely participate.

Hacking all night is fun, but you can also get some rest when you need it in our rest room!

Yes. Food and snacks are provided throughout the event, even overnight! (We know you night owls get the munchies.) There are also restaurants around the venue to buy food and snacks, and you can always bring your own!

We will start check-ins for free accomodation by MARCH 28th 4:00 pm GMT+0530 but you have to check-out by MARCH 30th 1:00 pm GMT+0530.

Hackathons are very beginner friendly. Whether you're an absolute beginner, a new computer science student, or an experienced hacker, you're the right fit for RECKON. Artists, musicians, athletes, gamers, everyone is welcome!

You can reach venue on your own convinience or reach to JODHPUR's selected checkpoints, JIET buses will pick you.Futher details will be given to you by mail if you are selected.

Bring a valid student ID or government-issued ID for admission and a laptop for hacking. We’ll provide pretty much everything else—meals,snacks,toiletries, stickers, sponsor swag, and more! If you like to be comfy, you can bring a blanket, pillow, and towel.

We will provide you free WIFI/LAN ,for better experience try to bring your own.

Registration are open to all students(school/college),professionals,startups etc can surely participate.
