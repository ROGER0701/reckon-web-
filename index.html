What is Reckon?

The third edition of RECKON is back, and we look forward to welcome creative and innovative minds to participate in this 36-hour hackathon taking place at Jodhpur Institute of Engineering and Technology to hack and have fun by solving some real-world problems.

RECKON provides you with the most precise platform if you believe in the power of transforming ideas into solutions. Our vision is to make students believe in their ideas and fabricate reality.

It doesn't matter if someone is still making their first app or their 100th one. There are a lot of things to learn.

The only constraint is time.

What is RECKON?


Reckon is a hackfest and can be best described as “Invention Marathon”. Anyone who has an interest in technology can attend this to learn, build and create.

What is the goal of RECKON 3.0?


With the belief in providing market-fit solutions we want to create an environment that embraces new ideas and innovations to make this world a better place.

What if it’s your first Hackathon?


Experience does not matter if you have the zeal to change the world. It’s helpful to have some programming or technical knowledge, but it is not a prerequisite.

How many members can a team have?


Your team can have a minimum of three and maximum of four members.

What do you need to carry along for the event?


A college/school ID card, laptops, chargers, hardware requirements (if any), all your energy and a great idea to change the world.

What are the prizes?


You have a chance to win prizes upto 3 lacs and cash prize worth 1.5 lacs.

Food, accomodation and travel reimbursement be provided?


Food, accommodation are for sure but *travel reimbursement will be partially provided to top 25 teams only.
