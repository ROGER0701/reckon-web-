What is Reckon?

The third edition of RECKON is back, and we look forward to welcoming soon all the creative and innovative minds to participate in this 36-hour hackathon taking place at Jodhpur Institute of Engineering and Technology to hack and have fun by solving some real-world problems.

RECKON provides you with the most precise platform if you believe in the power of transforming ideas into solutions. Our vision is to make students believe in their ideas and fabricate reality.

It doesn't matter if someone is still making their first app or their 100th one. There are a lot of things to learn.
 Our vision is to make students believe in their ideas and fabricate reality.

The only constraint is time.
